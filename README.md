# 💫 About Me:
Hey 👋 I’m Anugrah, a B.Tech student at Sahrdaya College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) who believes great technology comes from strong collaboration. I enjoy building digital solutions that create meaningful experiences, connect people, and solve real problems. Working with teams and engaging with tech communities has shown me that the best innovations happen when different perspectives come together.<br><br>I’m currently studying Computer Science and spend much of my time experimenting with code, building projects, and participating in hackathons. I enjoy taking rough ideas and shaping them into functional systems. Many of my projects begin from curiosity, sometimes becoming simulations, web apps, small games, or experimental tools, each giving me a chance to explore new technologies and creative approaches.<br><br>Hackathons are where I enjoy building the most, turning ideas into working prototypes within tight timelines. The projects here reflect that mindset of experimenting, learning, improving, and occasionally breaking things along the way. Right now I’m focused on strengthening my CS fundamentals while building projects around web technologies, simulations, and problem solving. Outside academics, I enjoy collaborating on new ideas and exploring projects that help me learn something new. If you enjoy building cool things or experimenting with technology, feel free to connect or collaborate !
